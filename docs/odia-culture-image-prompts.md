# Odisha/Odia Culture Image Generation Prompts

These prompts are designed for generating photorealistic images showcasing Odisha's rich culture, heritage, traditions, and landmarks. Use these with Google Nano Banana, Sora, getimg.ai, Midjourney, or similar AI image generation tools.

## Important Guidelines

### Key Specifications:
- **Camera Type:** DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Nikon D850, or Sony A7R IV)
- **Lens:** 24mm (wide), 50mm (standard), or 85mm (telephoto)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 to f/5.6
- **ISO:** 100-400
- **Style:** Photorealistic, ultra-realistic, high-resolution photography
- **Quality:** Professional travel/cultural photography style

### Essential Elements:
- Authentic Odisha cultural elements
- Natural lighting (golden hour preferred for monuments)
- Professional composition
- Realistic textures and details
- Cultural accuracy

## Technical Specifications for All Images

### Camera Settings:
- **Camera Type:** Professional DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Nikon D850, or Sony A7R IV)
- **Lens:** 24mm (wide), 50mm (standard), or 85mm (telephoto)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 to f/5.6
- **ISO:** 100-400 (higher for festivals/events: 800)
- **White Balance:** Daylight or custom (warm tones: 4800K-5500K)

### Image Quality:
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160px) or higher
- **Format:** High-quality JPG or PNG
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Style:** Photorealistic, ultra-realistic, no illustration
- **Quality:** Professional travel/cultural photography

### Composition Guidelines:
- **Rule of Thirds:** Apply consistently
- **Leading Lines:** Use architecture, natural elements
- **Depth of Field:** Vary based on subject
- **Framing:** Natural, professional framing

### Lighting Requirements:
- **Natural Light:** Preferred (golden hour for monuments)
- **Color Temperature:** Warm tones (4800K-5500K) for most, cooler for formal settings
- **Quality:** Soft, diffused lighting
- **Festival Lighting:** Vibrant, colorful for celebrations
