# Student Stock Photo Generation Prompts

These prompts are designed for generating photorealistic stock photos of school students in various educational settings. Use these with Google Nano Banana, Sora, getimg.ai, Midjourney, or similar AI image generation tools.

## Important Guidelines

### Key Specifications:
- **Camera Type:** DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Nikon D850, or Sony A7R IV)
- **Lens:** 24mm (wide), 50mm (standard), or 85mm (telephoto)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 to f/5.6
- **ISO:** 100-400
- **People Count:** 2-4 students maximum (preferably 2-3)
- **Style:** Photorealistic, ultra-realistic, high-resolution photography

### Essential Elements:
- Natural eye appearance (eyes looking at books/screen, not camera)
- Realistic books with natural wear
- Authentic school uniforms or casual clothing
- Natural lighting and shadows
- Professional photography style

## Category 1: Students Studying in Library

### Prompt 1: Two Students Studying Together
```
Ultra-realistic DSLR photograph, Canon EOS R5, 50mm standard lens, f/2.8 aperture, ISO 200, natural window light. Medium shot, eye-level camera angle, shot from 7 feet distance.

Scene: Modern school library with two Indian students (ages 15-17, one male, one female) sitting side-by-side at a wooden study table, studying together. Both students are looking down at their books or laptop screen, not at camera. Their eyes are naturally focused on their study materials with realistic gaze direction and authentic Indian eye characteristics, no AI eye artifacts.

Books on table: Two to three realistic books with natural wear - slightly worn edges, authentic page textures, realistic cover designs, natural shadows, not overly perfect. Books positioned naturally on the table. Laptop or tablet visible showing educational content.

Foreground: Students in sharp focus, study materials naturally arranged - books, notebooks, pens, highlighters. All objects have realistic textures and natural shadows.

Background: Floor-to-ceiling bookshelves with naturally arranged books of various colors and sizes. Large windows providing soft natural light. Educational posters on walls. Background softly out of focus but recognizable.

Lighting: Soft, diffused natural light from large windows, creating even, flattering illumination. Warm color temperature (5200K). Natural shadows adding depth. No harsh highlights.

Composition: Students positioned using rule of thirds. Study materials creating natural composition. Medium depth of field - students and foreground sharp, background soft.

Eye details: Both students' eyes naturally looking at books or screen, realistic eye shapes, natural reflections, authentic gaze direction, no AI eye artifacts, no uncanny valley effect, genuine studying expressions.

Details: Students wearing school uniforms (white shirts, dark pants/skirts) or modern casual clothing. Natural expressions of concentration. All textures realistic - wood grain, fabric, paper. Ultra-realistic, photorealistic style, shot with professional DSLR camera, high-resolution photography, documentary style.

Negative prompts: Avoid AI artifacts, perfect books, unnatural eyes, cartoon style, illustration, too many people (more than 3), cluttered scene, overly polished appearance, perfect symmetry.
```

## Technical Specifications for All Images

### Camera Settings:
- **Camera Type:** Professional DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Nikon D850, or Sony A7R IV)
- **Lens:** 24mm (wide), 50mm (standard), or 85mm (telephoto)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 to f/5.6
- **ISO:** 100-400
- **White Balance:** Daylight or custom (warm tones: 4800K-5500K, cool for exams: 6000K)

### Image Quality:
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160px) or higher
- **Format:** High-quality JPG or PNG
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Style:** Photorealistic, ultra-realistic, no illustration
- **Quality:** Professional photography, magazine-quality

### Composition Guidelines:
- **Rule of Thirds:** Apply consistently
- **Depth of Field:** Vary based on shot (shallow for portraits, medium for groups)
- **Framing:** Natural, professional framing
- **People Count:** 1-3 students (preferably 2)

### Lighting Requirements:
- **Natural Light:** Preferred for most settings
- **Classroom Light:** Bright, even for educational settings
- **Color Temperature:** Warm (4800K-5500K) for most, cool (6000K) for formal exams
- **Quality:** Soft, diffused lighting for flattering results

## Essential Elements for All Prompts

### Must Include:
1. **People Count:** "Two Indian students" or "One to three students" (never more than 4)
2. **Eye Details:** "Eyes naturally focused on [subject], not at camera. Realistic eye appearance, natural gaze direction, no AI artifacts"
3. **Book Details:** "Books with natural wear, realistic textures, authentic printing quality, not overly perfect"
4. **Clothing:** "School uniforms" or "casual modern clothing"
5. **Negative Prompts:** Always include at the end

### Common Negative Prompts:
```
Negative prompts: Avoid AI artifacts, perfect books, unnatural eyes, cartoon style, illustration, too many people (more than 4), cluttered scene, overly polished appearance, perfect symmetry, uncanny valley effect, artificial textures, unrealistic lighting, AI-generated appearance.
```

## Usage Tips

1. **Choose Appropriate Setting:** Match the prompt to your website's needs
2. **Maintain Consistency:** Use similar camera specs and style across all images
3. **Diversity:** Vary student appearances, clothing, and settings
4. **Quality Check:** Verify no AI artifacts, realistic eyes, and natural book appearance
5. **Optimize for Web:** Resize and compress images after generation
